The Metamorphosis

Franz Kafka

Finished: April 2021

Thoughts

It was a very quick read. I struggled with its meaning, but after some searching around on the internet I realise that it shows the difficulties of living in a modern society and the struggle for acceptance of other when in a time of need.

Quotes

“I cannot make you understand. I cannot make anyone understand what is happening inside me. I cannot even explain it to myself.”
