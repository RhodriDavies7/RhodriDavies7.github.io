Lord of the Flies

William Golding

Finished: February 2021

Thoughts

It was good. I was really captivated throughout. I wouldn't say that the ending was a let down, but perhaps it would've been better if the 'adults' didn't save them at the end. I was more expecting Ralph to die and for the true nature of Jack to be amplified further. Despite this it was still very good, and one I will read again sometime.

Quotes

“We did everything adults would do. What went wrong?”
