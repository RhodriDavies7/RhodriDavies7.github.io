Wuthering Heights

Emily Bronte

Finished: April 2021

Thoughts

I'm glad that I read it, however it was a very frustrating book. It is a great love story in the sense that Heathcliff is completely and utterly in love with Cathy, but the toxicity of the situation was at times very hard to overlook. As I said, I'm very glad that I read it but I don't see myself reading it again in the future.

Quotes

“He's more myself than I am. Whatever our souls are made of, his and mine are the same.”

“If he loved with all the powers of his puny being, he couldn't love as much in eighty years as I could in a day.”
